Key Insight

  • Repeating the same promotional message across all countries will increase CAC while conversion rates stagnate, necessitating country-cluster-specific offer design.
  • The key to performance improvement this week is separating and executing country-specific acquisition (new) and defense (churn/returns) targets.

Action Required

  1. For price war countries (🇺🇸 US/🇲🇽 MX/🇸🇦 SA), set conversion rate defense as the #1 KPI and immediately execute perceived price correction offers on flagship TV SKUs.
  2. For promotion optimization countries (🇩🇪 DE/🇫🇷 FR/🇮🇹 IT), execute CRM retargeting within 7 days after promotion end to re-engage cart abandonment customers.
  3. For growth pioneering countries (🇹🇭 TH/🇹🇼 TW/🇸🇬 SG/🇪🇬 EG), prominently feature entry-level SKUs with interest-free/installment benefits to focus on expanding the new customer pool.

  2. Unify customer communication scripts for the top 3 complaint types (repair delay, response delay, warranty confusion) to reduce re-complaints at the consultation stage.
  3. Send installation/initial defect check messages to customers within 14 days of purchase to preemptively absorb early issues.

3.4 Chinese Brand Threat Tracking (16 Countries)

Key Insight

  • Chinese brands win not through low prices but through purchase journey preemption. They gain exposure first on search page one and bestseller sections, preempting the consideration set.

  • The solution is not standalone price response but execution that increases early search stage share of voice (search/retail exposure/comparison messaging).

Action Required

  1. In 🇺🇸 US/🇸🇦 SA/🇦🇺 AU/🇲🇽 MX, immediately execute LG vs TCL/Hisense comparison keyword search ads and comparison landing pages to increase consideration set entry rate.
  2. For models facing intense low-price competition, combine trade-in/interest-free installments instead of direct discounts to secure conversion without ASP decline.
  3. Negotiate with retailers to increase LG core SKU exposure share in bestseller/recommendation slots and track weekly performance.

4. Chinese Brand Threat Report

Key Insight

  • TCL targets the volume zone while Hisense targets the lower-premium segment, simultaneously pressuring LG's new customer acquisition channels from both sides.
  • If this dual pressure continues, LG's mid-price segment market share will likely decline first, regardless of its premium image maintenance.
  • The defense core is not across-the-board price cuts but productizing value propositions with quantifiable metrics (warranty period, installation speed, service SLA).

Action Required

  1. Design monthly joint promotions with retail partners in 🇺🇸 US/🇸🇦 SA/🇦🇺 AU to clearly differentiate LG-exclusive benefits (installation/warranty/gifts).
  2. Attach Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) messaging to core TV SKUs to clearly convey that maintenance cost/service benefits are superior even if initial purchase price is higher.
  3. In segments with large gaps vs Chinese brands, prominently place second-best SKUs (value-oriented LG models) to absorb churning customers within the internal lineup.
